Indonesian Journal of Chemistry

The Indonesian Journal of Chemistry is an open access journal that publishes original research articles, review articles, short communications in all areas of chemistry, including educational chemistry, applied chemistry, and chemical engineering. This journal is indexed in Scopus and DOAJ.

Quality selected papers from Material Science and Chemistry symposium will be published in Indonesian Journal of Chemistry (Q3 Scopus indexed journal by Department of Chemistry UGM).
