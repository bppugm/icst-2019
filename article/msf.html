Materials Science Forum

Materials Science Forum is a peer-reviewed journal which covers all aspects of theoretical and practical research of materials science: synthesis, analysis of properties, technology of materials processing and their use. Materials Science Forum specializes in the publication of thematically complete volumes from international conference proceedings and complete special topic volumes.

We're pleased to announce that selected papers from Material Science and Chemistry symposium of ICST 2018 have been published in the Materials Science Forum. Browse the papers in the ICST 2018 website or visit the Materials Science Forum website to read and download them.

ICST 2019 has been approved by Materials Science Forum with conference record number #4863.
